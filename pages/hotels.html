Objective

Undertaking the task of revamping the hotels portal on the company's website to provide a refreshed and enhanced user experience. This effort involves the incorporation of innovative features aimed at elevating the overall functionality and usability of the portal, aligning with the evolving needs of our users

Design Process
User Insights Utilization

Leverage user research findings, market trends, and competitor analysis to understand user behavior, needs, and pain points on the current portal.

Feature Prioritization & Ideation

Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define and prioritize new features, drawing inspirations from user feedback and industry best practices

Concept Visualization

Translate feature ideas into wireframes and interactive prototypes, ensuring a seamless and intuitive navigation flow that aligns with user expectations.

Aesthetic Enhancement

Employ a harmonious blend of the existing brand identity and modern design elements, creating visually captivating interfaces that engage users.

Collaborative Development

Work closely with development teams, ensuring the design vision is translated into functional code that maintains responsiveness and cross-device compatibility.

User-Centric Validation

Organize user testing sessions to validate the design's effectiveness, gather qualitative insights, and make necessary adjustments to improve user interaction.

Iterative Refinement

Iteratively refine designs based on user feedback, maintaining a continuous feedback loop and aligning the design with the evolving needs of the users.

Ongoing Enhancement

Continuously gather user feedback, analyze usage patterns, and collaborate with the team to iteratively enhance the portal's design and functionality for optimal user experience.
